Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026 | Effective date: April 7, 2026

1. Introduction

Study Tips, operated by Sling Corp. ("we", "us", or "our"), is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our web-based analytics service ("the Service"). By using the Service, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information through the TikTok Display API only after you explicitly authorize access via TikTok's OAuth 2.0 process. The specific data we collect includes:

2.1 Profile Information (via user.info.profile scope):

  • Display name
  • Username
  • Profile image URL

2.2 Video Performance Data (via video.list scope):

  • Video titles and descriptions
  • View counts
  • Like counts
  • Comment counts
  • Share counts
  • Video publish dates

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect:

  • We do not collect your TikTok password
  • We do not collect private messages or direct messages
  • We do not collect information about your followers or following lists
  • We do not collect demographic data about your audience
  • We do not collect any financial or payment information
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To display your content performance analytics within the Service
  • To generate comparative insights across your video content
  • To identify trends in your content engagement over time
  • To provide actionable recommendations for content optimization

We do not use your data for advertising, marketing to third parties, or any purpose other than providing you with analytics insights.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share your personal information or analytics data with any third parties. We may disclose information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • When required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Sling Corp., our users, or the public
  • With your explicit written consent

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is processed locally in your browser session and is not permanently stored on our servers. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit using HTTPS/TLS protocols. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only for the duration of your active session with the Service. When you close the Service or revoke access, your data is no longer accessible through our platform. Access tokens issued by TikTok are stored securely and are automatically invalidated when you revoke access through your TikTok account settings.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to Access: You can view all data we have collected about you within the Service dashboard.
  • Right to Revoke Access: You can revoke Study Tips' access to your TikTok data at any time by visiting your TikTok account settings under "Manage app permissions."
  • Right to Deletion: Upon revoking access, all associated data will be removed from our systems. You may also contact us directly to request data deletion.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You may withdraw your consent to data collection at any time by discontinuing use of the Service and revoking API access.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a child, we will take immediate steps to delete such information.

9. International Data Transfers

As the Service processes data locally in your browser, cross-border data transfers are minimized. However, API calls to TikTok's servers may involve data transfer to servers located outside your country of residence. These transfers are governed by TikTok's own Privacy Policy and data protection practices.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the Service after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
