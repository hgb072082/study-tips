Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Study Tips collects the following information through the TikTok Display API with your explicit authorization:

- Basic profile information (username, display name)
- Video performance metrics (view count, like count, comment count, share count)
- Video metadata (title, description, publish date)

2. How We Use Your Information

All collected data is used solely for personal content performance analysis. We do not sell, share, or distribute your data to any third parties.

3. Data Storage

Data is stored locally on the user's device and is not transmitted to external servers.

4. Data Deletion

You may revoke access to your TikTok data at any time through your TikTok account settings. Upon revocation, all associated data will be deleted.
